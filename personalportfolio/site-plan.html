Content

Home page

The personal portfolio website for Logan Gauruder is made to highlight his many tech-related areas of expertise. This includes experience in web development, programming, and cybersecurity. As a tech enthusiast and aspiring Cybersecurity professional, Logan has developed a diverse skill set through academic pursuits and some practical applications through means of personal projects. He is currently studying Computer Science, at both Brigham Young University Provo and Brigham Young University Idaho, and is working on receiving certifications such as CompTIA Security+, which will position him as a versatile professional ready for roles in security analysis. Visitors of this webpage, such as employers, recruiters, and potential clients in the tech industry, will come to review and assess Logan's qualifications and explore collaboration opportunities. This site addresses key questions that they may have for Logan. These questions are as follows: What are Logan's core skills and experiences? Which projects demonstrate his capabilities? How can I contact him for job discussions or freelance work? By already providing clear, concise answers to these important questions, the site streamlines the hiring and connection process. This portfolio offers authentic, first-hand insights into Logan's personal and professional work projects, a more intimate self-marketing approach than found on common job websites. It features in-depth project breakdowns, including challenges faced and solutions implemented to overcome them in each project's development. This gives visitors a more comprehensive sense of Logan's problem-solving capabilities. The structure of the site aims at simplicity: The home page gives an overview of Logan's biographical information, including some background and skills. The projects page gets into specific examples of past or ongoing projects with visuals and links to showcase his work. The work experience page includes contact information for easy outreach and a resume with key tech-related experience. This site not only showcases technical proficiency but highlights Logan's passion for innovation and drive for continuous learning. In a rapidly evolving field like cybersecurity, these qualities position him as a reliable candidate who stays updated on trends like AI-driven security and full-stack development. Visitors should be able to leave with a clear understanding of his value.

Projects

Activities on the site include navigating through detailed project showcases and viewing code repositories on GitHub. A standout project that he worked on is a Python script he made for interaction with Grok xAI to suggest LinkedIn professionals to connect with on a regular basis. He also recreated a Texas Hold'em poker game in Python, demonstrating his ability to build interactive applications. He enjoys building algorithmic solutions, like pathfinding programs for game development hobbies. Cybersecurity is the main focus of Logan's professional development. He's knowledgeable in areas such as penetration testing, network configuration, threat modeling, and databases. He has completed projects in vulnerability testing, cryptography, database building, cloud server hosting, and AI security. In one simulation, he identified and mitigated SQL injection risks in a mock web app, which demonstrates basic proactive defense strategies.

Work Experience

This page focuses on Logan Gauruder's previous work experience relating to tech, specifically a focus on cybersecurity. Contact information is available as well as resume items which can be downloaded in PDF format. Visitors, whether employers, recruiters, or potential clients can reach him by submitting messages via an integrated contact form. Essential information like email, LinkedIn profile, and location details ensure seamless communication and a user-friendly experience. For potential local Utah-based opportunities, the site emphasizes Logan's familiarity with Utah regional tech ecosystems. In web development, Logan excels in creating user-friendly interfaces utilizing HTML, CSS, JavaScript. His programming skills cover languages such as Python, Java, and C++, with applications in automation, data analysis, and software tools. Logan has experience in cybersecurity from his ongoing education at Brigham Young University Provo and Brigham Young University Idaho, where he has developed skills in penetration testing, network security, and threat modeling. He is currently pursuing certifications like CompTIA Security+ to further enhance his expertise in the field. Previous job experience includes 3 different call centers consisting of a HIPAA-compliant medical intervier position, gift card technical support, and legal firm representative compliant to HIPAA and other privacy regulations. This has helped Logan to develop skills pertaining to interpersonal soft skills, familiarity and expertise with call support softwares in addition to Microsoft Teams, Zoom, and other team communication platforms, customer service skills, goal-orientation, team communication, and problem-solving. He has also worked in Chiropractic offices, which has given him experience in organization, time management, attention to detail, and compliance to HIPAA and other privacy regulations. Logan also served for 2 years for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints as a full-time missionary in the Texas Dallas East Mission. This experience helped him to develop skills in communication, training, problem-solving, partnership/team coordination, marketing/presentation, adaptability.
